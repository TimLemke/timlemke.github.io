About

Hello!

My name is Tim Lemke and I am professional guitarist and teacher based out of London Ontario Canada.

I have had the privledge to perform with some of Canada’s most prominent musicians, and I have also taught hundreds of students. I am comfortable teaching all styles of music to students of all ages and levels.

I have received both a Master of Music and a Bachelor of Music in Jazz Performance with Honors from the University of Toronto. I studied there with some of Canada’s finest jazz musicians including Mike Murley, David Braid, Geoff Young, and David Occhipinti among others. In addition, I also studied classical guitar with renowned classical guitarist and teacher Dr. Jeffery McFadden. I also hold a Certificate in Jazz Guitar Performance from New York University, and a Diploma in Music Performance with Distinction from Grant MacEwan University where I studied with jazz guitarists Jim Head and Jamie Philp.
