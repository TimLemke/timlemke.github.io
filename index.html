About Me

Hello!

Thanks for checking out my site. My name is Tim Lemke, and I am full stack web developer living in and working remotely from London ON. I have over four years of professional experience and have worked in both a B2B SaaS environment and agency setting. Personally, I feel most comfortable in any setting where inclusivity, teamwork and a good sense of humour can be found.

As a developer I always love learning something new and delivering the best code I can every time. That means code that performs well, is maintainable and is accessible to everyone. My professional experience includes the application of numerous languages, tools and technologies across a wide range of industries. This experience allows me to jump into any project and hit the ground running, contributing from day one.

I am self-managed, hard working and thrive in environments where creativity and teamwork are key, new ideas and concepts must be quickly learned and applied, and where it is important to share with others along the way.

When I am not writing code you can find me hanging out with family and friends, exploring local coffee shops, going for a hike, or searching for another cool LEGO minifigure to add to my collection.

Thanks for stopping by!
